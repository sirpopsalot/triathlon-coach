# Workout Library

## Glute + Hip Routine (25 min total)

### Strength Block (15 min)

| Exercise | Sets/Reps | Equipment | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-----------|-------|
| Sumo deadlift | 3x10 | Barbell, moderate weight | Wide stance, toes out |
| Banded clamshells | 1x15/side | Band around knees | Slow and controlled |
| Single-leg RDL | 1x10/side | 30 lb kettlebell | Hinge at hip, flat back |
| Kettlebell swings | 3x15 | 30 lb kettlebell | Hip snap, not arm pull |
| Banded lateral walks | 1x15 steps/direction | Band around ankles | Stay low, don't waddle |

### Mobility Block (10 min)

| Stretch | Duration | Notes |
|---------|----------|-------|
| Half-kneeling hip flexor | 60s/side | Squeeze glute, tuck pelvis |
| 90/90 stretch | 60s/side | Both legs at 90 degrees, rotate torso |
| Couch stretch | 45-60s/side | Back foot on wall or couch |
| Pigeon pose | 60s/side | Keep hips square |

## Zwift Workouts

### Zone 2 Easy (45 min)
- 5 min warmup, gradually increasing
- 35 min steady Zone 2 (conversational pace)
- 5 min cooldown

### Intervals (45 min)
- 10 min warmup
- 4x3 min pointed effort (Zone 4) with 2 min recovery
- 10 min cooldown

## Run/Walk Progression

| Week | Pattern | Total Time |
|------|---------|------------|
| 1-2 | 1 min run / 2 min walk | 20 min |
| 3-4 | 2 min run / 1 min walk | 25 min |
| 5-6 | 3 min run / 1 min walk | 30 min |
| 7-8 | 5 min run / 1 min walk | 30 min |
| 9+ | Continuous easy running | 30-40 min |

## Aqua Jog (25-30 min)

- Use flotation belt
- Deep end, no ground contact
- Maintain running form
- Effort: conversational to moderate
- Counts as run training with zero impact

## Swim Sessions

### Monday Coached (60 min)
- Follow coach's workout
- Focus on technique corrections

### Midweek Solo (45 min)
- 200 warmup (mix of strokes)
- 4x100 freestyle with 20s rest
- 4x50 drill/swim by 25
- 200 cooldown

## Long Ride Nutrition

- Eat breakfast 2-3 hours before
- Bring: 1 bottle/hour, bars/gels every 45 min
- For rides 3+ hours: real food (PB&J, banana)

## Volume Targets by Phase

### Phase 1: Century Build (Feb-Apr)
- Bike: 80-150 mi/week
- Run: 30-60 min/week (including aqua jog)
- Swim: 2 sessions/week
- Strength: 3 sessions/week

### Phase 2: Alcatraz Build (Apr-Jun)
- Bike: 60-80 mi/week
- Run: 60-90 min/week
- Swim: 3 sessions/week
- Strength: 2 sessions/week
